About Samuel Vasquez

Samuel Vasquez is a photographer and Graphic Designer. His love for design has led him to work with many companies including LightGrid, Global Moonshots in Education, and 23andMe. Samuel has taught Design as Discovery at Stanford University and has been named a Stanford McGee Fellow. He collaborates with us as a web designer and Graphic Design Consultant.

Here is a sample of his digital photography portfolio. We are excited to have Samuel Vasquez on board and to include him as one of our World Wide Wave Collaborators.
